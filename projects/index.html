Residential Driveway Transformation

Project Type: Residential Driveway
Location: Christchurch
Finish: Exposed Aggregate
Duration: 3 Days

The Challenge

This residential driveway project involved transforming an aging, cracked concrete surface into a modern, durable exposed aggregate driveway. The client wanted a finish that would complement their home's contemporary architecture while providing excellent durability for Christchurch's variable weather conditions. The existing driveway had significant surface deterioration and poor drainage, requiring complete removal and reconstruction.

Our Approach

Our team began by carefully removing the existing surface and assessing the underlying base. We prepared the foundation to council specifications, ensuring proper compaction and drainage. The exposed aggregate finish was selected to match the home's aesthetic while providing excellent grip and weather resistance. Custom stone selection allowed us to create a unique finish that enhances the property's curb appeal.

The Result

The completed driveway exceeded the client's expectations, providing a beautiful, low-maintenance surface that enhances the property's value. The exposed aggregate finish offers excellent durability and requires minimal ongoing maintenance. The project was completed on schedule and within budget, with the client expressing satisfaction with both the quality of work and our professional approach.

"The team at I Pave transformed our driveway beautifully. The exposed aggregate finish looks fantastic and has held up perfectly through Christchurch's weather. Professional service from start to finish."

— Christchurch Homeowner

Custom Concrete Patio Installation

Project Type: Concrete Patio
Location: Canterbury
Finish: Stamped Concrete
Size: 80m²

The Challenge

This project involved creating a large outdoor entertaining area with a custom-stamped concrete patio. The client wanted a design that mimicked natural stone while providing the durability and low maintenance of concrete. The site required careful leveling and integration with existing landscaping, including coordinating with garden beds and outdoor structures.

Our Approach

We worked closely with the client to select the perfect pattern and color combination that would complement their home's exterior. The installation process included proper site preparation, base compaction, and careful placement of expansion joints to prevent cracking. The stamped pattern was applied with precision to create a natural stone appearance, followed by color application and sealing for long-term protection.

The Result

The finished patio provides a stunning outdoor living space that the family enjoys year-round. The stamped concrete finish successfully mimics natural stone at a fraction of the cost, while offering superior durability and minimal maintenance. The project was completed on time, and the client was thrilled with both the aesthetic result and the functionality of their new outdoor space.

"We absolutely love our new patio! The stamped concrete looks exactly like natural stone, but it's so much easier to maintain. The team was professional, clean, and completed the work exactly as promised."

— Canterbury Homeowner

Commercial Parking Lot Paving

Project Type: Commercial Paving
Location: Christchurch
Size: 2,500m²
Duration: 2 Weeks

The Challenge

This large-scale commercial project required paving a new parking lot for a retail complex. The challenge was completing the work with minimal disruption to existing businesses while meeting strict deadlines and quality standards. The site required coordination with multiple tenants, traffic management during business hours, and adherence to council requirements for commercial parking facilities.

Our Approach

Our team coordinated with multiple stakeholders, including property managers, tenants, and council representatives. We developed a phased work schedule that allowed businesses to remain operational throughout the project. Work was scheduled during off-peak hours where possible, with clear communication to all parties. The concrete mix was specifically designed for heavy traffic loads, and we implemented proper drainage systems and expansion joints to ensure long-term durability.

The Result

The project was completed on time and within budget, with excellent drainage and durability for heavy traffic. The new parking lot meets all commercial standards and provides a safe, functional surface for customers and employees. Business operations were minimally disrupted, and the property owner was extremely satisfied with both the quality of work and our professional project management approach.

"I Pave handled our commercial parking lot project with exceptional professionalism. They coordinated seamlessly with our tenants, completed the work on schedule, and the quality is outstanding. Highly recommend for commercial projects."

— Property Manager, Christchurch
