Beautiful Decorative Concrete Finishes in Christchurch

Decorative concrete allows you to achieve the look of premium materials like stone, brick, or tile at a fraction of the cost. Our expert team creates stunning decorative finishes that enhance your property's aesthetic appeal while maintaining concrete's durability and low maintenance.

Our Decorative Concrete Options

Choose from a variety of beautiful finishes

Stamped Concrete

Create patterns that mimic natural stone, brick, or tile. Stamped concrete offers endless design possibilities with the durability of concrete.

Colored Concrete

Integral color mixed throughout the concrete provides consistent, fade-resistant color that lasts for decades.

Exposed Aggregate

Reveal natural stone aggregates for a textured, non-slip surface that's both beautiful and functional.

Polished Concrete

A sleek, modern finish perfect for contemporary homes. Polished concrete is easy to maintain and highly durable.

Stenciled Concrete

Custom stenciled patterns and designs allow for unique, personalized decorative finishes.

Acid Stained Concrete

Create unique, marbled effects with acid staining. Each project is one-of-a-kind with rich, variegated colors.

Benefits of Decorative Concrete

Decorative concrete offers numerous advantages:

  • Cost-effective alternative to natural stone or tile
  • Durable and long-lasting in all weather conditions
  • Low maintenance compared to other decorative materials
  • Endless design and color options
  • Increases property value and curb appeal
  • Can be applied to new or existing concrete
  • Environmentally friendly option
