Our Services

One contractor. All concrete solutions. Built to last.

With over 45 years' experience across Canterbury, IPave delivers professional concrete and asphalt services for homes, businesses and specialist civil projects. From a tidy residential driveway to large commercial slabs and precision kerbing, we handle every job from design through to completion, no shortcuts, no surprises

Commercial Paving

When it's time for car parks, warehouse floors or industrial access ways, our commercial-grade concrete paving is built to perform. With planning, mixing, reinforcement, control joints and precision finishing, we deliver slabs that meet heavy-duty demands and commercial standards.

Kerbing Services

Precision kerb and channel installation defines edges, manages runoff and completes the look of your project. From driveways and gardens to commercial boundaries and stormwater control, we install kerbs and channels with accuracy and long-term performance.

Create beautiful outdoor living spaces with custom concrete patios designed for entertaining, relaxation and long-term value. From design consultation through to finishing touches, we deliver patios that complement your home and withstand Canterbury's climate.
