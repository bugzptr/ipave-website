Residential Driveway FAQ

Q: How long until I can drive on my new driveway?

A: You can usually walk on it within 24 hours. We recommend waiting 7 to 10 days before driving a standard vehicle on it to allow the concrete to gain sufficient strength.

Q: Do you handle the Council permits and drainage requirements?

A: Yes. We ensure all our residential driveways meet local Council requirements for water runoff and crossing specifications.

Q: How do I maintain my concrete driveway?

A: We recommend a professional seal every 2-3 years, especially for exposed aggregate and coloured concrete, to keep the finish looking vibrant and protect it from oil stains.
