About iPave Concrete & Asphalt

With nearly 50 years of hands-on experience, IPave has grown from a one-man operation into one of Christchurch's most trusted concrete teams.

Founded by Daryl McLachlan, who started concreting at just 16, IPave is built on real experience, family values, and genuine craftsmanship. From driveways and patios to large-scale commercial work, every project is delivered with pride, precision, and care.

Over the years, IPave has helped shape Canterbury, completing more than 22,000 jobs during the Christchurch rebuild and now operating in both Christchurch and Auckland. As a family-run business, with Daryl's son Brook and a loyal crew of long-serving team members, iPave continues to pour the same dedication into every job because for us, it's more than concrete. It's community.
