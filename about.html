Our Story: Built on Experience, Crafted with Care

Every great business starts with a foundation and for Daryl McLachlan, that foundation was laid when he picked up a concrete trowel at just 16 years old. What began as a teenage job quickly became a lifelong craft, shaping a career that now spans nearly five decades.

Our Journey Through the Years

From humble beginnings to Christchurch's trusted concrete specialists

The Early Years

Daryl first honed his skills working with Duncan Grey and Enterprise Homes, learning the trade inside and out. This foundation of knowledge and craftsmanship would become the cornerstone of IPave Concrete's success.

Taking the Leap

28 years ago, Daryl took a leap of faith, starting out on his own with just one staff member and a big vision. From those humble beginnings, IPave Concrete has grown into a trusted Christchurch name.

Building a Team

Today, IPave is supported by a loyal team many of whom have worked alongside Daryl for 15, even 20 years. This loyalty and dedication is what sets us apart in the industry.

The Christchurch Earthquakes

The team's biggest test came in the aftermath of the Christchurch earthquakes. At the height of the rebuild, IPave completed more than 22,000 jobs in just seven years, with close to 100 staff on the ground.

Expanding to Auckland

Never one to stand still, Daryl expanded into Auckland eight years ago after spotting a gap in the market. Today, he continues to run a crew of expert concrete placers there.

A Family Business

At its core, IPave Concrete is more than a business, it's a family. Daryl's son Brook is part of the team, and the long-serving staff feel just as much like family too.

What Makes Us Different

It's this loyalty, craftsmanship, and genuine pride in every job that has made IPave Concrete a name you can trust for quality concrete work across Christchurch and beyond.
